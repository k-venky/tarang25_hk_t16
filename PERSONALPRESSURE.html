Dealing with Personal Pressure

  • Practice mindfulness to reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Set aside time for journaling to organize your thoughts.
  • Limit screen time and spend more time outdoors.
  • Focus on positive self-talk to combat negative thinking.
  • Stay connected with family and close friends for emotional support.
